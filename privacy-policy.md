# Trakio Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

## Overview

Trakio is designed to be local-first. Most session data stays on your device unless you choose to share something, use a third-party integration, or contact us directly.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Trakio stores the following data locally on your device:

- track data
- sessions
- laps
- sector times
- GPS samples
- session notes
- car information
- weather snapshots saved with sessions
- your local driver profile

Your local profile may include:

- username
- car
- nationality / country code
- avatar image
- a local publisher ID used for optional leaderboard sharing

## Permissions We Use

### Location

Trakio uses location permission to record GPS telemetry and detect lap and sector crossings during track sessions.

### Photo Library

Trakio uses photo library access only if you choose to:

- select an avatar image
- choose a photo for story sharing
- save a generated image to your photo library

If you deny these permissions, the related features will not work.

## Network Services

Trakio may contact the following services over the network:

- **Open-Meteo** to fetch current weather for a selected track
- **Hosted leaderboard service** if you choose to share a lap time
- **Instagram** if you choose to share a session to Instagram Stories
- **Your device mail app** when you send feedback by email

## Leaderboard Sharing

Leaderboard sharing is optional.

If you choose to share a lap, Trakio sends the following data to the hosted leaderboard service:

- track ID
- shared username
- country code
- car
- lap time
- submission date
- a stable local publisher ID so your time can be updated later on the same track

Shared leaderboard entries are intended to be visible to other users.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Local sessions, notes, profile data, and cached app data remain on your device until you edit or delete them, or remove the app.

Shared leaderboard entries may remain on the hosted leaderboard even if you later delete the related local session from your device.

Trakio does not currently provide an in-app self-service removal tool for hosted leaderboard entries.

## Third-Party Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

Trakio does not currently use:

- third-party advertising SDKs
- user tracking for advertising
- mandatory account login
